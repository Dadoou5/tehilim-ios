An app born of love, dedicated to a memory.

Tehilim was created to carry the memory of Johann Meïr ben Sarah Bouganim — a young man whose story and smile inspired so many, including the film The Specials (Hors Normes).

Every psalm read in the app is offered for the elevation of his soul. Every letter lit is a candle. Every prayer becomes a gesture — for him, for those who remain, for those we love.

ה' שֹׁמֵר אֶת כָּל אֹהֲבָיו
"The Lord preserves all who love Him." — Tehilim 145
Features

The whole Book of Psalms, rethought.

From daily reading to personalized sequences, every detail is designed to accompany prayer without ever distracting from it.

Daily Tehilim

The monthly Psalms cycle aligned with the Hebrew calendar. Pick up exactly where you left off — the app remembers everything, from the day to the letter, from the verse to the translation.

5 books

All 150 Tehilim, structured by the traditional division into five books. Pointed Hebrew, faithful English translation, fluid navigation.

Life cases

Marriage, birth, healing, parnassa, success, mourning… For every moment, a traditional selection of Tehilim, with the customary prayers before and after the reading.

Personalized reading

Compose a sequence of verses from a first name: Refoua Chelema for healing, Lelouy Nichmat in memory of a loved one. The app generates the letters and their verses — and computes the next azkara, with reminders 7 days ahead and on the day.

Tehilim ChainNew

Read the 150 Tehilim, together.

A real-time group reading for a shared intention — Lelouy Nichmat, Refoua Chelema, or the success of a loved one. Everyone picks the Tehilim they commit to read, with no duplicates, wherever they are.
